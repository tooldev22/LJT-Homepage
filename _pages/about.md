I am a first-year Ph.D. candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.ust.hk/), [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://hkust.edu.hk/), supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://junxian-he.github.io/). 

Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. degree from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024, where I was awarded the Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship.

My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Machine Learning (ML)**. Specifically, I am interested in:
*   **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
*   **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
*   **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**

I have also gained research experience through internships at [MINIMAX](https://www.minimaxi.com/) (Feb 2025 - Present), [Tencent WXG](https://www.tencent.com/en-us/) (Jun 2024 - Sep 2024) advised by Zifei Shan, and [Shanghai AI Lab](https://www.shlab.org.cn/) (Jun 2023 - Dec 2023) advised by Prof. Yu Cheng.
